Dan Bing - Traditional Taiwanese Egg Roll

Description

Dan Bing (蛋餅) is a popular Taiwanese breakfast dish! It´s basically a thin, chewy, slightly crispy pancake wrapped around an egg and any other ingredients that you would like to add. In this recipe I will add Bacon and Cheese, but feel free to choose your own fillings!

Ingredients

  • Frozen pancakes (can be bought in asian supermarkets)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of oil (for frying)
  • Filling options: shredded cheese, cooked bacon

Steps

  1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a non-stick frying pan on medium heat
  2. While heating the frying pan, crack an egg in a bowl and stir it
  3. Add the egg to the frying pan and put the frozen pancake on top of it
  4. After 1-2 minutes turn the pancake with the egg around (egg should be on top now)
  5. Add the fillings (in this case shredded cheese and cooked bacon) to your liking
  6. Fold/roll the pancake, fry it a bit from all sides and serve it
  7. (Optional): If you like it a bit crispier, fry the rolled up dan bing a bit longer and serve it.
